Hello, my name is Scott Davis. I am originally from Missouri. I live in Utah now.
My favorite food is shrimp. I like movies, chocolate, and an occasional hike.
But really chocolate I can't live without!
Did you know that one cocoa bean contains 50% nib and 50% cocoa butter. It's also amazing to note that each cocoa bean has a specific origin and that speicific origin gives it a unique flavor. My favorite cocoa beans are from Madagascar, Ecuador and Belize. The Madagascar have a very fruity undertone to them. They taste like a raspberry or cherry even. Whereas Ecuador has more of a nutty flavor to it. The reason for this is because the cocoa butter takes in that which is around it. In Ecuador, in the canopy above the cacao trees grow almond trees. These flavors mix with the cocoa butter and at harvest time these flavors come out in a very unique and beautiful way. It's been amazing all that I've been able to learn as a self taught chocolate maker. I used to hate dark chocolate because it was chalky and was way too bitter. But after creating my own, I can't get enough of it.
