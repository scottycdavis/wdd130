Trip Packages

Trip 1: River Adventure

Experience the thrill of navigating through challenging rapids and breathtaking scenery on our River Adventure trip. This full-day excursion offers adrenaline-pumping excitement as you conquer the rapids and marvel at the natural beauty surrounding you.

Trip Highlights:
  • Challenging rapids
  • Stunning scenery
  • Professional guides
  • Lunch included

Trip 2: Solo Expedition

Embark on a multi-day Solo Expedition where you'll raft through remote wilderness areas, camp under the stars, and immerse yourself in the untouched beauty of nature. Our self-guided river maps will lead you on an unforgettable journey through some of the most pristine landscapes in the region.

Trip Highlights:
  • Multi-day adventure
  • Remote wilderness
  • Camping under the stars
  • Wildlife sightings

Trip 3: Family Fun Rafting

Looking for a fun-filled adventure suitable for the whole family? Join us for our Family Fun Rafting trip, where you'll enjoy gentle rapids, beautiful scenery, and plenty of opportunities for swimming and splashing. Our guides will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for rafters of all ages.

Trip Highlights:
  • Family-friendly rapids
  • Scenic river views
  • Swimming and splashing
  • Safe for all ages
